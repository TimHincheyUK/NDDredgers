Training Sessions

Swimming

Our members join swim clubs, in the local area, that have sessions catering to all abilities, from beginners to advanced triathletes. These tend to be paid sessions.

We organise open water swimming sessions in the summer months. These are free of charge.

We also have a number of members who are qualified swim coaches and can help with technique.
